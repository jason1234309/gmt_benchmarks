2.Trojan
  
Trojan Description
	The Trojan increases clock frequency in a part of circuit. The Trojan is triggered upon observing 0xFF over the address bus for bits 0-7. 
	The Trojan consists of 7 cells that are distributed across the layout. 

Trojan Taxonomy
	Insertion phase: Design
	Abstraction level: Layout-level
	Activation mechanism: Internally time-based triggered
	Effects: Denial of service
	Physical characteristics: Loose, Functional, and Layout-same
